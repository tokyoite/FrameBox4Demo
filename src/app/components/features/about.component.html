About FrameBox.js

FrameBox.js was designed to be an open source framing engine for Fine Art websites to incorporate beautiful framing into their online galleries. We also provide a secondary framing service for e-commerce websites that are interested in selling high quality framing with their artwork. Please contact us by email at tavig123@hotmail.com  if you are interested or have any questions regarding our API.
